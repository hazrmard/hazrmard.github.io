About Me

Hi. My name is Ibrahim. I have a degree in Physics and Electrical Engineering and I am a MS/PhD student at Vanderbilt University.

I have done projects related to machine learning, mobile and web development, electronics, high performance computing, and modelling.

My last research project was on the mysterious dark matter halos inside our galaxies and how their interactions can be better explained.

I aim to contribute to mankind's next leap into exploration of our universe. Coming up with the Grand Unified Theory would cross another item off my bucket list.
