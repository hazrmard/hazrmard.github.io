About Me

Hi. My name is Ibrahim. I am a Masters student at Vanderbilt University with a concentration in Electrical Engineering (EE). I also have a double major in EE and physics.

I am interested in hardware & software design, machine learning, web & mobile development, and astrophysics.

Currently my research is on adaptive fault tolerant systems using reinforcement learning. Prior to that, last summer, I was involved with making sense of terabytes of data from simulations on Dark Matter.

I like to play tennis, badminton, and video games. Also, In my free time, I read fiction, cook (because no meal plan for grad students :(), and work on personal projects.

I aim to contribute to mankind’s next leap into exploration of our universe. Coming up with the Grand Unified Theory would cross another item off my bucket list.
